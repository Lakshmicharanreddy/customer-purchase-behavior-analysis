# Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis Using E-Commerce Data

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using an e-commerce dataset to uncover patterns in customer activity, sales trends, product performance, and seller contributions. The objective is to generate actionable insights that help improve customer retention, optimize product strategy, and enhance operational efficiency.

The analysis combines SQL-based data exploration with Python-based data visualization to understand key drivers of revenue and customer satisfaction.



## Business Objectives
The analysis aims to answer the following key business questions:

- How is the overall business performing in terms of orders and revenue?
- What sales trends exist over time?
- What customer purchasing patterns can be observed?
- Which products and categories drive the most sales?
- How do sellers contribute to marketplace performance?
- What factors influence customer satisfaction?



## Dataset
Dataset: Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist

The dataset contains multiple relational tables representing an e-commerce marketplace.

Main tables used in this analysis include:

- Customers
- Orders
- Order Items
- Payments
- Products
- Product Categories
- Sellers
- Reviews
- Geolocation data

## Key Insights

### Customer Behavior
Most customers make a single purchase, indicating an opportunity to improve customer retention through loyalty programs and targeted marketing strategies.

### Sales Trends
Order volume and revenue fluctuate over time, with noticeable peaks during certain periods, suggesting seasonal purchasing behavior.

### Product Performance
A limited number of product categories generate the majority of sales, highlighting key product segments that drive marketplace performance.

### Seller Contribution
A small number of sellers account for a large portion of marketplace revenue, indicating a reliance on high-performing sellers.

### Customer Satisfaction
Customer ratings are generally positive; however, delayed deliveries are associated with lower review scores.



## Business Recommendations

- Implement customer retention strategies such as loyalty rewards and personalized promotions.
- Increase marketing focus on high-performing product categories.
- Strengthen relationships with top-performing sellers while supporting smaller sellers.
- Improve delivery logistics to reduce delays and improve customer satisfaction.
